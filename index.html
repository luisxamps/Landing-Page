Some advices about life

When something makes you feel different and you realize it, dont rush in to doing something impulsive action just trying to break that "bad" feeling. Instead of that give yourself some time to process, alone, in silence, in the gym, doing whatever you want but take some time to process and make some decision on that.

Drugs

Being alone is like a drug, I would say it has some hallucinogenic effects and induces sensations that you would never experience otherwise. In small and well-managed doses, loneliness can heal wounds, bring positive feelings, and result in a peace of mind due to the absence of the usual chaos of everyday life. The problem is that you don't always have control over the dosage; it's not a typical drug. The peculiarity is that the people around you also have the power to choose how much of this drug will be in your system. As for the effects felt in cases where the drug is mismanaged, it would be an understatement to compare them to hell itself.

Sadness

Being sad isn't that bad, on the contrary that I thought for years. The endless run from depression turns you into a ansious and unstable person, who doesn't accept any bad feeling afraid that feeling those for a while will bring back the dying wish and the anguish of living. I believe that be able to feel sad and doesn't fall for that is a way to be truly strong, like staring at the abyss without the fear of being stared back by it, trusting in yourself that you will not feel the needy to jump on it. Or swimming in a sea full of monster and bad creatures trusting that they won't eat you alive(or just do not care if they will or not).

Loneliness

The problem about spend so much time alone with yourself is that the only voice you will be able to hear is the voice of your mind, and once it realizes that you're a good listener, it will never shut up again. It talks too much.
